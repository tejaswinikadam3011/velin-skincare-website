ABOUT VELIN

The Story Behind the Bottle

We believe skincare should be simple, honest, and made with care — not complicated, not rushed.

OUR ORIGIN

Where It All Began

Velin began with a simple frustration — most skincare labels were full of ingredients no one could pronounce, wrapped in promises no one could trust. We wanted something different: formulas we'd feel comfortable explaining to anyone who asked.

So we started small. Every product is mixed in tiny batches, tested carefully, and refined slowly — never rushed just to keep up with trends.

Today, Velin is still handmade, still small-batch, and still rooted in that same idea we started with: your skin doesn't need more, it needs better.
